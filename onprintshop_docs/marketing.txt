To make it easier for customers to find your products on search engines like Google and Bing, the OnPrintShop control panel has spaces where you can insert material expressly for SEO.
Content pages, category pages, product pages, blog entries, and brands all include fields for page names, descriptions, and keywords.
The best methods to maximize your SEO impact are covered in this area, along with information on where to enter SEO-related metadata.
