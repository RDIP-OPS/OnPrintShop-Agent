Introduction
1 min
We mainly offer two kinds of products: printing products and Ready To Buy products.
Printing Products
We provide different types of Products such as business cards, banners, and so on, all of which are free of charge. Another service we provide is add-on products such as canvas prints and photo prints. These are paid products.
Ready To Buy Products
These products are ones that do not require much customization and are ready to sell, such as a cardholder or a desk clock. Admin can add these products both individually and as a Kit. A kit is a collection of two or more products.
